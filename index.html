Abstract

I am a Computer Science Lecturer (roughly equivalent to a US Assistant Professor) at the University of Sydney, and the co-founder of D23E.ch. I focus on security and privacy problems. I work closely with Professor Arthur Gervais and Dr Kaihua Qin.

A significant part of my work focuses on Decentralized Finance (DeFi) due to their abundant data (code, transactions, attacks) and transparency. With a high Total Value Locked (TVL~$96 billion), research in this area has a substantial impact.

My ultimate goal is to develop generalized AI in security, similar to how Noam Brown created the first Poker AI.
